Introduction

EchoDrama: Bridging Cultures Through AI

At EchoDrama, we believe that great stories deserve a global audience.
As a pioneering startup, we specialize in using cutting-edge AI technologies to localize Asian short dramas for international markets—seamlessly and authentically.

Whether it's a heartfelt romance from Seoul or a suspenseful thriller from Bangkok,
we ensure that the emotional depth, cultural nuances, and artistic integrity remain intact as stories cross borders.
Our mission is to empower creators and media platforms to share their content with the world—without losing its original flavor.

Projects

AI-Powered Subtitle Translation

We provide high-quality, context-aware translations for Southeast Asian and Middle Eastern languages, ensuring accuracy, cultural relevance, and natural flow for each line of dialogue.

Voice Cloning for Dubbing

EchoDrama’s voice cloning technology replicates the original actors' tone, cadence, and emotional expression in multiple languages—bringing characters to life across cultures without losing their essence.

Automated Video Reconstruction

Our system synchronizes translated subtitles and dubbed audio to generate fully localized videos that are ready for distribution across global platforms, with minimal human intervention.
